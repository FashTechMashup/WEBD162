Barking Mad by Suzi Webster

Suzi Webster created a coat that helps people know when to stay out of your bubble. The coat seems ordinary until you approach it and hear soft barking. Step closer and the sound of an aggressive growl will greet you. Watch the short video to your right and check out a demo of it in action.
