Iridescence by Behnaz Farahi

This piece is what inspired me to attend Wired to Wear in the first place. I'm so happy I did because there were dozens more of wearable tech pieces for me to experience once I got there. Iridescence contains facial-tracking cameras in its colorful collar that shifts according to your position and mood. So moving to your left and frowning would cause the 200+ panels to react differently than if you approached the piece laughing and to the right.
