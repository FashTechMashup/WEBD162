Spider Dress by Anouk Wipprecht

I have been a fan of Anouk's 3D printed fashion for a while now, so it was a pleasant surprise to see one of her pieces at the exhibit. Like Barking Mad, this article of clothing is meant to protect the wearer from unwanted attention. The spider like legs will "push" intruders away if someone comes into close contact to the wearer or approaches too quickly.
