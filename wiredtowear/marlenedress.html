The Marlene Dress by Lisa Lang

I’ve always been a sucker for bright and sparkly things and this dress has both with its LEDs and Swarovski crystals. A combination of 3D printing, laser cutting, and traditional fashion methods brought this dress to life. It’s hard to see from the picture but it looked like the crystals themselves were glowing. My favorite part of this dress though is the inspiration. The designer Lisa Lang was inspired to make this dress after finding letters by an actress named Marlene Dietrich who, “wrote of her desire for a dress that glowed softly and left people wondering if they had imagined seeing light.”
